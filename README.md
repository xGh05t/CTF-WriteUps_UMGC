# CTF-WriteUps_UMGC

A collection of Capture The Flag (CTF) write-ups from competitions completed during my time at the **University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC)**.

Challenges were attempted both solo and as part of a team, covering categories like forensics, cryptography, web exploitation, reverse engineering, and more.

## Structure

```
01-MetaCTF_Jan/        MetaCTF — January 2026
02-LoveBytes_Feb/      LoveBytes CTF — February 2026
03-HackTheMadness/     Hack The Madness — March 2026
04-MarylandCTF/        Maryland HTB CTF — April 2026
05-MarylandCTF_2/      Maryland HTB CTF — May 2026
```

Each folder contains the original challenge artifacts and a `WALKTHROUGH.md` documenting the solution.

## Disclaimer

These write-ups are for **educational and research purposes only**. All challenges were completed in authorized CTF competitions. Techniques shown here should never be applied to systems you do not own or do not have explicit permission to test. The author assumes no responsibility for misuse of this material.
